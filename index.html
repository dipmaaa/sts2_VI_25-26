A. Multiple Choice

Pilihlah jawaban yang paling tepat!

1. I ... go to Bali next school holiday.

2. They ... play football in the field tomorrow afternoon.

Rina : What are you doing, Sam?
Sam : I am preparing my bag. My family and I ... visit our grandmother in Bandung tonight.

3. The best word to fill the blank is ...

4. Based on the text above (no. 3), Sam ... visit his grandmother tonight.

5. She ... not come to the party because is sick.

6. ... you help me bring these books?

7. I think it ... rain this afternoon.

8. We ... learn English together next Monday.

Next Sunday, my school will hold a cleaning day. All students will bring cleaning tools. We will clean the classroom and the garden. After that, the teacher will give up some snacks.

9. Next Sunday, the students ... clean the school.

10. Based on the text above, the teacher ... give the snacks to the students after cleaning.

B. Fill in the Blanks

Isilah titik-titik dengan "will" atau "will not (won't)" + kata kerja dalam kurung!

11. My father ... (buy) a new car next month.

12. I ... (study) hard for the final exam tonight.

13. Don't worry, I ... (help) you with your homework.

14. The phone is ringing. I ... (answer) it.

15. They ... (watch) a movie at the cinema this weekend.

16. ... you ... (join) the scout activity tomorrow?

17. It's very cold here. I ... (close) the window.

18. My sister ... (cook) fried rice for breakfast tomorrow morning.

19. We ... (visit) the zoo if we have enough time.

20. I am tired. I ... (go) to sleep early tonight.
